Jason Shatsky is the CEO & Co-founder of TicketRev--a reverse marketplace for event tickets-- which in short terms is a platform that makes it cheaper for concert/show-go-ers to find tickets to their favorite live events. When COVID arose, the concert and show industry took a huge hit, despite this discouraging environmental shift, Jason kept resilient and worked his a** off to make TicketRev what it is today.
