Through every stage and season of your life, you meet a lot of different people.

The people I dedicate this website to are not only incredibly influential to me, but they are consistenlty kind-hearted and influential to others.
I took time to reflect on how I got so lucky to be surrounded by these "one-in-a-million" type of people...
In short, this reflection showed me that I was gifted with the permanence of good people with good hearts in my life.

There is so much to learn about yourself and the world from everyone you cross paths with.
The lessons I have learned makes me feel deeply appreciative for those who leave deep influences on me.
I now know that I am who I am because of the incredible people in my life.
I wouldn't be who I am today & I wouldn't have acheived the things I have achieved without my family, friends, and mentors.

This website is a daily reminder to tell everyone in your life that you are grateful for them.
Life feels good when you surround yourself with inspiring, kind, and ambitious humans.
